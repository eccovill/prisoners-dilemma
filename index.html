Prisoner's Dilemma Game

Rules

  1. You and an accomplice have been arrested and are being interrogated separately.
  2. If neither of you confess, you each receive one year in prison.
  3. If only one of you confesses, the confessor goes free and the other receives three years in prison.
  4. If you both confess, you both receive two years in prison.
